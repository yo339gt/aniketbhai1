About Us

The Studio

The quest for absolute quality implies that we follow a unique method of design development. We imbibe the skills & experience of the people who actually make the buildings & objects that we design. Their involvement acknowledges them as equal stakeholders in the process, & allows us to better predict construction outcomes & quality control.

How We Work

Current projects include commercial & industrial buildings, corporate offices, hospitality, boutique retail, multifamily housing, private houses & furniture. ANIKET endeavours to employ technologies that sustain rather than pollute, that are durable rather than replaceable, and that add value over time rather than falling prey to short term economies.

Our Services

Luxury Design

The team includes the best designers and the best architects who have great creative potential and many years of experience in the field of luxury design and architecture.

Residential Design

Our projects encompass architectural design of myriad types of homes such as residences, bungalows, villas, row houses.

Commercial Design

We also offer unique designs for commercial complexes, buildings, shopping malls and arcades. We can help you make your dreams a reality.

Landscape Design

A complete solutions provider, Aniket undertakes responsibility for all design elements, including landscape, structured design and other production utility services.
